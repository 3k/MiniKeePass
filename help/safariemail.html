Opening files from Safari or Email

MiniKeePass can open KeePass files from Safari, Email, or any other app that can access KeePass files.

Safari:

  • In Safari, click on a link to a password database anywhere on the web.
  • Click on Open in "MiniKeePass"

Email Attachments:

  • In the Email app, click on the password database attachment.
  • Click on Open in "MiniKeePass"
